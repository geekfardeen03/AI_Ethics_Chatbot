Bias in AI occurs when models produce unfair outcomes due to skewed training data.
To mitigate bias, use diverse datasets and audit models regularly.

Fairness in AI means ensuring equal treatment across all groups.
It is important in hiring, lending, and healthcare applications.

Privacy in AI involves protecting user data and following regulations.
Sensitive information must not be misused.

Transparency means AI decisions should be explainable.
Users should understand why a decision was made.
